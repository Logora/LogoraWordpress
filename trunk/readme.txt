== Description ==

[Logora](https://logora.fr/) is the web’s most popular debate platform trusted by major publishers to increase reader engagement, grow audience and traffic, and monetize content. Logora helps publishers of all sizes engage directly with their audiences to build loyalty, retain readers, and foster thriving communities.

The Logora for WordPress plugin lets site owners and developers easily add Logora to their sites. Logora installs in minutes and lets you start debates easily.

= Why Logora ?=

* Simple one-click installation that seamlessly integrates with WordPress without ever needing to edit a single line of code
* Keep users engaged on your site longer with a debate experience readers love
* Bring users back to your site with web and email notifications and personalized digests
* Improve SEO ranking with user generated content
* Keep spam out with our best-in-class anti-spam filter

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I customize the look-and-feel of Logora ? =

You can customize Logora by going to your [Logora admin panel](https://admin.logora.fr).

= How do I set up Single Sign-On (SSO)? =

SSO allows users in your database to debate without requiring them to register with Logora. Access to SSO is available automatically with this plugin.
To enable SSO, don't forget your secret key in the Logora Wordpress settings.

= I’m experiencing an issue with my installation of Logora. =

Check out our [Documentation](https://docs.logora.fr) or contact us at support@logora.fr.

== Installation ==

1. Go to **"Plugins"** > **"Add Plugin"**
2. Search for **"Logora"** by Logora
3. Click **"Install Now"**
4. Activate the plugin
5. Enter your site's Logora shortname and API secret key in the **Logora settings page**. This requires you to have [created a Logora account](https://logora.fr/signup) and registered your site.
